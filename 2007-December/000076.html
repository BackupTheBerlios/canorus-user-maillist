Canorus is still in development, our plan is to have a version 1.0 having
at least the features NoteEdit has now. For daily use Lilypond is currently
the only export possibility, though we hope that MusicXML import comes soon.
Also printing and hopefully preview too will be ready until january. I plan
on working full time next week on printing (and it's preparation hencefor).

Concerning the 3E: Really a nice Subnotebook/UMPC. I have so far heard of
quite impressive performance, I still wait for some real report about audio
and midi performance with a RT kernel. For score editing I'd recommand an
(external) larger display though.

As Matevz told for production work currently NoteEdit is certainly more
advanced. As Canorus will get import functions for NoteEdit, MusicXML and
other formats before or at 1.0 there's no need to worry about future of this
program.
