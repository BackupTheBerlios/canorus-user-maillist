BTW: NoteEdit's native export format is strongly based on the MUP syntax. 
Mup and Mupmate can handle NoteEdit files (*.not) directly. If you know 
the mup syntax you can read and edit NoteEdit files manually with any 
editor. But after such editing use mup and not noteedit to manage the 
score.
