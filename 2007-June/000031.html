> Use +/- keys for adding/removing accidentals.
Hmmm... ok. I played around with +/- and see whats happen.

When you pressed the - key, the shadow note shows one b for normal notes and 
a double b for notes that have already a b from the key signature. But when 
you klick a note with a single b or withot any b is inserted.

Another nice thing: when you press +/- multiple times, the #/b of the shadow 
note disappears, but you can enter notes as asasasasasasas in this way, 
since you have no feed back, how often the - key has been pressed.
