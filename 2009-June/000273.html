Hi,

I've encountered some bugs (version R1144):

(De)crescendo signs can't be extended. In edit mode, the mouse cursor turns to 
a double arrow over the end of such a sign (which is marked with a black 
point), but it can't be dragged.
(De)crescendo, dynamics, ritardando and tempo marks are not exported to 
LilyPond / shown in print preview.
Copy&paste of notes does not work properly in staffs with more than one voice. 
If you already have notes in one voice and want to paste notes in the other 
voice, the result is a mess. Maybe the reason are the invisible rests which, 
on inserting notes on one voice, are automatically inserted in the other 
voices.
