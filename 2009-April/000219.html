it works well, thanks for that fast implementation. There is only a small
inconsistency between Canorus and the Lilypond output whether the fermata is
displayed above or below the line. Canorus puts the fermata sometimes above
and sometimes below the line according to some rule intransparent to me,
whereas Lilypond always puts it above (which seems more reasonable to me).
However, the in my opinion most prefarable way would be to let the user
choose, e.g. by allowing to toggle the place (above or below) by
right-clicking the fermata. The default could be determined from where the
stems in the current voice point to (i.e. below for always down, above for
always up or automatic).
