> I'm planning to implement the Tab/Shift-Tab keys to switch between the

do you mean Tab for going to the next voice/staff and Shift-Tab for going to 
the previous voice/staff? That would be much better than it is now. Anyway I 
think arrow keys would be more intuitive for that; as you go to the 
next/previous note with right/left arrow, it would be only consistent to go 
to the next/previous voice or staff with down/up arrow. Changing pitch in 
edit mode could then be implemented with Shift-up and Shift-down (to prevent 
key binding collision).
