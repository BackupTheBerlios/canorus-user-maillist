We use Eclipse & Mingw (gcc for windows) for easier
cross-platform-ability. And gdb for debugging, but MSVC compiler and VS
debugger might work as well. You might download Qt library separately
and Canorus source and try to compile & debug it yourself. You might
also want to download some Qt examples to test SVG stuff only.

Check out developer's docs:
https://canorus.berlios.de/wiki/index.php/Developers_Guide
