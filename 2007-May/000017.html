Hi Helge,

The output below is fine - debugging only.

The vector SVG icons (the ones for music elements insertion) problem is
known and we haven't found a solution yet. On my laptop for example,
icons work flawlessly on both clean WindowsXP+SP2, Windows2000+SP4 and
Wine. However, on my father's Dell laptop, icons don't work on both
WindowsXP+SP2 and Wine. So something is really weird here and I think
it's more of a Qt/Microsoft/Driver bug than ours. The funny thing
however is, that bitmap PNG icons work fine everywhere.


Regards.
