Hi,

I just list some mentor organization which could fit for Canorus projects.
Any students who want to apply with a Canorus project should check these
first if it fits for their project and then apply it there directly.

Here is the list of all organizations:
http://socghop.appspot.com/program/accepted_orgs/google/gsoc2009

We will of course help you if any problems arise in submitting your project.

So here is the list (uncommented):
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/asf
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/berkman
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/codehaus
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/fsij
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/karlberry (GNU)

Please do not forget also to look at the homepage of every organization
and study the projects they are doing. Also the list of organization
table has a project ideas page you have to take a look at.

Here are some less fitting organizations with comments:
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/abiword (possible
integration into that app ?)
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/ccan (for something
special lowlevel probably core but still useful as generic C (not C++) lib)
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/gnome (that would
actually be a challenge ;)
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/haskell (Haskell
plugin ?)
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/mejmeeks (see
abiword)
http://socghop.appspot.com/org_app/show/google/gsoc2009/mozilla (special
Mozilla extension ? Too special IMO)
