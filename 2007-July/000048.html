Users:

I have written up some thoughts on improving usability in Canorus. The 
thoughts and suggestions in the article are based on my personal 
experience with notation editors, casual observation of other users, and 
the simple principle of reducing unnecessary modes.

In computers a mode is "a distinct setting...in which the same user 
input will produce perceived different results than it would in other 
settings" (Wikipedia). Caps lock and insert keys are two common 
examples. Although modes are sometimes necessary, they also mean users 
are likely to make mistakes, forget which mode they are in, or not 
understand "why it is not working like its supposed to." These are 
called "mode errors."

I believe a discussion of usability is important to Canorus and this 
article is my contribution to that discussion. The views expressed in 
the article are my own. I am open for feedback.

The article:
http://davidbolton.info/articles/notation_interface_design.html
