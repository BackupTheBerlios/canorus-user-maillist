Hello Nathan,

Sure it's true we are no longer active on the NoteEdit channel. You
can find some of use on the Canorus channel instead. I'm not always
online myself but someone there should be able to help you. Alternatively
you can try one of the forums in case of troubles.

The main things for making Canorus quite useable are missing triples,
drums, guitar tabulature input and printing/preview. The last one is
currently in development by me and basic printing and preview are close
to be implemented. As I wrote on the wiki you will need the poppler library
with qt4 support as Qt does not offer printing from file.
